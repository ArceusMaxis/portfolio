About Me

E

Who Am I

Hello! I'm a passionate game designer with a deep love for creating immersive, memorable gaming experiences. My dream is to craft worlds that players would want to explore and stories they'd remember long after the credits roll.

With a background in both design and development, I bring a unique perspective to every project, especially in regards to UI, Level and Mechanic design.

F

My Work

I specialize in game UI, to create interfaces that are both intuitive and visually engaging. Additionally, I have a strong focus on level design, crafting environments that enhance gameplay.

My design philosophy centers on giving players agency while not holding back on challenging gameplay. I make games both solo and with teams, and I participate often in game jams to keep my skills sharp.

G

My Dreams

Looking ahead, I aspire to push the boundaries of interactive storytelling and create games that leave lasting emotional impacts. I believe games are the most powerful medium for empathy and connection.

My ultimate goal is to lead creative teams in developing innovative experiences that push creative boundaries and deliver meaningful gameplay. I'm committed to bringing diverse cultural perspectives and inspiring others through my work.
