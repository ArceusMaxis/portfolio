Moros

A remake of the hit game FLUOROANTIMONIC in the Godot Engine. The player collects offerings to appease a hungering god whose ichor spills from the skies as hazards. Solo-developed in a very small time span and it features PACKAGE ENCRYPTION [DRM], which was an interesting challenge to implement in Godot.
